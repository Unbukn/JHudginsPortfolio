About Me

Mr. James Hudgins is Full Stack Web Developer with over 8 years of experience, during which he has provided system design, software development, testing, certification, business case analysis, and functional audit support to several significant United States Navy submarine programs. As a System Design Engineer with Patrona Corporation, Mr. Hudgins combines development knowledge with his ship design background to engineer solutions that allow rapid transformation required for continuous mission success. Mr. Hudgins is steadfast in the development of secure, modular, DRY applications, even when presented with complex challenges introduced by working in antiquated Department of Defense (DoD) environments.
